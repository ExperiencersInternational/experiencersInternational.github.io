List of social platforms for high profile individuals or media outlets in the tech and gaming industries on Mastodon

This site contains a list of most news outlets, companies or individuals who are high profile in the tech community. I try to remain as accurate as possible, but if you're a tech or gaming nerd, hopefully this'll help you. For most of my initial tech related stuff, this is taken from Windows Centrals article on here's who to follow on Mastodon. Planning to add meta tags soon, sorry if the website looks awful, I'm not the best HTML coder, just grabbed it off a template I made for another project on the same domain.

Most individuals are identified with having their account in this colour. Some may be excluded however.

Guide to labelling: 1: Mostly Twitter posts but no automation is ran on the account to my knowledge, 2: One to one Twitter mirrors, 3: Most, if not all content is original (identified to the best of my ability), 4: Unknown.

If you don't want to make the move to Mastodon, RSS is supported on most servers, but if you're viewing accounts that aren't on the server you're viewing on, go to the person's page on their own server and add .RSS to the end of their URL.
